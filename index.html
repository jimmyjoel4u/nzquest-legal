NZQuest

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4 July 2026

NZQuest ("the app", "we") is a New Zealand family days-out guide published by NoviaAI. This policy explains what information the app handles and where it goes. The short version: NZQuest has no accounts, runs no servers of its own, and never sells your personal information. The one third party that processes data in the app is our advertising provider, described in the Advertising section below.

What the app stores — and where

Everything you do in NZQuest is stored only on your device:

  • your selected and starred (favourite) regions,
  • bookmarked deals,
  • trips you build and save in the route planner,
  • cached listings fetched from public event sources,
  • a flag recording that you've seen the welcome screens.

None of this is transmitted to us. We have no servers and no way to see it. Deleting the app's storage (Android Settings → Apps → NZQuest → Clear storage) or uninstalling the app permanently removes all of it.

Network requests the app makes

The app talks to the internet only to show you content:

  • Event listings are fetched from public sources (for example Eventfinda's and Auckland For Kids' public endpoints).
  • Images for activities and trips are loaded from their providers' servers (for example Google's place-photo service and content networks).

Like any internet request, those services receive your IP address and standard request information, handled under their own privacy policies. NZQuest sends them no identity, account, or profile information — it has none to send.

Links to partner sites

Deal cards open partner websites (BookMe, GrabOne, First Table, TreatMe, Everything NZ, Eventfinda and similar) in your browser. Once you're on a partner's site, their privacy policy and terms apply. NZQuest does not sell anything itself and no purchase information flows back to the app.

Some outbound links may in future include an affiliate reference so the partner knows the visit came from NZQuest. This tells the partner nothing about you personally.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app can alert you about price drops on bookmarked deals and new listings in your starred regions. These checks run entirely on your device. You can turn notifications off at any time in Android settings, per channel.

Sharing

Trip sharing is entirely user-initiated: when you share a trip, the text or image goes only to the app you choose in the share menu. Nothing is shared automatically.

Children

NZQuest is designed for parents and caregivers planning family activities. It is not directed at children, and it collects no personal information from anyone, of any age.

Advertising

NZQuest is free and supported by advertising, served by Google AdMob. Ads are clearly labelled "Sponsored". To deliver and measure ads, Google may process device information such as your IP address and your device's advertising ID, under Google's Privacy Policy and its advertising technology policies. NZQuest itself does not receive this data.

  • Where the law requires it (for example EEA/UK visitors), the app shows a consent screen before any ads load.
  • You can reset or delete your device's advertising ID, or opt out of personalised ads, at any time in Android Settings → Google → Ads.
  • NZQuest is an app for parents and caregivers; ads are not directed at children.

Analytics

Beyond the advertising described above, the app contains no analytics or tracking SDKs. If crash reporting or usage analytics are added in a future version, this policy will be updated first and the Play Store data-safety listing updated to match.

Your rights

NZQuest is offered from New Zealand and we support the principles of the NZ Privacy Act 2020. Because the app keeps no data about you, requests to access or delete personal information are satisfied by the on-device deletion steps above. If you have any privacy question, contact us and we'll help.
